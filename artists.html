Biography

Years Attended: 9

Burning man projects and affiliations:

Throughout my years at the burn, I’ve been involved in a variety of projects. As a TCO I’ve spoken at, and helped to organize, the annual conference. As part of the CAMP Support team I mentor other camps that are new and/or have challenges. With that, I also support Yeti in the logistics of Camp Support. With the Cultural Direction Setting team I worked on the GANNT chart for the various burn committees. For art, I’ve built on the temples twice off playa, and once on playa, as well as built on the lighthouses and the Folly. On playa I served as a docent for the Folly, and was part of a team that kept it from burning prematurely.
